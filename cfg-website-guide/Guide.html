Our Guide

Here are our best tips for what to prepare yourself with before you travel

Front Loading Backpack
A front loading backpack means you can open the entire front of the bag, just like a suitcase. This is easier than rummaging through the top of your bag, trying to find the things at the bottom.

Packing Cubes
packing cubes make packing and finding your stuff way easier. They are also a good way of keeping your dirty clothes separate.

Point it Dictionary
a point-it dictionary will make getting around easier when you are in a foreign country or you don't know the language. It can be helpful when ordering food or looking for a place or thing that you don't know the word for.
